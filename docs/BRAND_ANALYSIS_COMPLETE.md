**Brand Name:** The Faithful Mindset  
**Tagline:** "Today is a beautiful new day"

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Design:** Four-petal flower/butterfly symbol in vibrant magenta (#D91184)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, botanical
- **Symbolism:** Growth, transformation, beauty

### Color Palette

**Primary Background:**
- Crisp White (#FFFFFF)
- Soft pink/cream backgrounds for warmth

**Accent & Clickables:**
- Vibrant Magenta: #D91184 (primary brand color)

**Botanical Graphic Accents:**
- Charcoal Gray: #3B3B3B (RGB: 59, 59, 59)

### Typography
- **Script Font:** Elegant, handwritten style for "Declarations of [Theme]"
- **Serif Font:** Clean, modern serif for body text and subtitles
- **Sans-serif:** For navigation and UI elements

## Design Principles

### Visual Aesthetic:
1. **Soft & Calming:** Gentle, soothing floral imagery
2. **Clean & Minimal:** Lots of white space
3. **Elegant Typography:** Mix of script and serif fonts
4. **Botanical Focus:** Dahlias as primary floral element
5. **Color Psychology:** Each book color represents its theme

### User Experience Goals:
- Create feelings of safety and calm
- Visual-first experience (not text-heavy)
- Easy navigation and discovery
- Peaceful, healing atmosphere
- Professional yet approachable

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Tone:
- Warm and welcoming
- Hopeful and uplifting
- Gentle, not pushy
- Authentic and personal
- Inspirational without being preachy

### Key Messages:
- "Beautiful reminders for the heart"
- Visual healing through calming imagery
- Faith + neuroscience + action (subtle integration)
- Accessible emotional wellness
- "Today is a beautiful new day"
