# Brand Analysis - The Faithful Mindset

This document will be used to analyze the Marketing folder materials once uploaded.

## Brand Evolution

### Previous Direction
- Course-focused platform
- Instructional approach to emotional healing
- Required courage from users to "take a deep dive"

### New Direction (Garden of Declarations Series)
- Visual, image-based experience
- Soothing products that calm the mind
- Induces feelings of safety and encourages healing
- More accessible - users experience rather than commit to change
- Fun and uplifting for Hilary to create

## Core Philosophy

**Faith + Neuroscience + Action**

The blend remains, but delivery method shifts from instruction to visual experience.
