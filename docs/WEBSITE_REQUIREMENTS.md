# Website Requirements - The Faithful Mindset

This document outlines the technical and functional requirements for the new website.

## Project Goals

1. **Rebrand** from course-focused to visual/product-focused experience
2. **Showcase** The Garden of Declarations Series (12 books)
3. **Create** calming, soothing user experience aligned with healing mission
4. **Enable** mixed e-commerce (direct sales + external links)
5. **Support** future product expansion (journals, calendars, courses)

## Core Features

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero Section:** Visually stunning introduction to The Garden of Declarations
- **Brand Story:** Brief, compelling narrative about the new direction
- **Featured Products:** Showcase of the 12 books with beautiful imagery
- **Visual Experience:** Calming animations and transitions
- **Call-to-Action:** Clear path to explore products or learn more

### 2. Product Showcase
- **Individual Book Pages:** Dedicated page for each of the 12 declarations
- **Gallery View:** Grid or carousel of all 12 books
- **Product Details:** Description, themes, benefits
- **Purchase Options:** Mix of direct purchase and external links (Amazon, etc.)
- **Related Products:** Cross-promotion of journals, calendars, courses

### 3. About Section
- **Hilary's Story:** Personal connection to the work
- **Philosophy:** Faith + Neuroscience + Action explained visually
- **Mission:** Helping people experience healing through visual calm
- **Credentials/Background:** Establish trust and authority

### 4. E-Commerce Functionality
- **Mixed Approach:**
  - Some products: Direct purchase (Stripe/PayPal integration)
  - Some products: External links (Amazon, course platforms)
- **Shopping Cart:** For direct purchases
- **Checkout:** Secure payment processing
- **Order Management:** Basic order tracking

### 5. Course Integration
- **Course Listings:** 6-week course offerings
- **Framework Information:** What students can expect
- **Enrollment:** Link to course platform or direct signup
- **Course Materials:** Preview or sample content

### 6. Content Management
- **Blog/Resources:** Optional content marketing capability
- **Newsletter Signup:** Email list building
- **Social Media Integration:** Links and possibly feeds

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- **Fast Load Times:** Optimized images and code
- **Mobile Responsive:** Perfect experience on all devices
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- **SEO Optimized:** Proper meta tags, structured data, sitemap

### Design Principles
- **Calming Aesthetic:** Soft colors, gentle animations
- **Visual-First:** Large, beautiful imagery
- **Minimal Text:** Let visuals do the talking
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Easy to explore and discover
- **Whitespace:** Breathing room for peaceful experience

### Technology Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (React)
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (subtle, calming effects)
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **E-Commerce:** Stripe or Shopify integration (TBD)
- **CMS:** Headless CMS for content management (optional)
- **Hosting:** Netlify or Vercel
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics or privacy-focused alternative

## User Experience Flow

### First-Time Visitor
1. Land on homepage with stunning hero
2. Immediately feel calm and safe (visual design)
3. Discover The Garden of Declarations Series
4. Explore individual books or themes
5. Learn about Hilary and her mission
6. Choose to purchase, subscribe, or explore courses

### Returning Visitor
1. Quick access to new products or content
2. Easy navigation to favorite books/themes
3. Check for new courses or resources
4. Engage with blog/newsletter content

## Content Strategy

### Messaging Hierarchy
1. **Primary:** Visual experience of calm and safety
2. **Secondary:** The Garden of Declarations Series products
3. **Tertiary:** Faith + neuroscience + action philosophy
4. **Supporting:** Courses, additional products, resources

### Tone & Voice
- Warm and welcoming
- Hopeful and uplifting
- Gentle, not pushy
- Authentic and personal
- Professional but approachable
